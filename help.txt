WHAT THE DISPLAY MEANS:
	highlighted text: letter in correct spot
	underlined text: letter in word, but not correct spot
	regular text: letter not in word

TO SETUP A NEW GAME:
	run: ./init.sh {word-length}

TO MAKE A GUESS:
	run: ./makeGuess.sh {word}

TO CLEAN UP IF SOMETHING WENT WRONG:
	run: ./endGame.sh

TO PRINT CURRENT STATE OF GAME:
	run: ./printGame.sh
